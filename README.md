Jack of All Trades

Jack of All Trades is a web app designed to help you explore new skills, track your progress, and level up through achievements. Whether you’re learning programming, cooking, or juggling, the app lets you:

Pick skills you want to improve.

Track achievements tied to each skill.

Earn ranks and badges as you make progress.

See how you stack up against others with similar interests.

The goal is simple: motivate continuous learning, gamify self-improvement, and celebrate mastery—one skill at a time. Become a true Jack of All Trades!
